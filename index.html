Hello There!

I am a Research Scientist at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA) at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). My interests focus on developing software tools for scientific research with particular interest on computational modeling in astronomy and plant biology. I am a Co-PI for the Crops in Silico project, the primary developer for the yggdrasil package, and a contributor to the yt project. I am particularly interested in making connections across disciplines and finding new avenues for cross polination between disciplines.
